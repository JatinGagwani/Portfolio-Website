ABOUT JATIN

Data Analyst to
Marketing and Business Guy
(Still Figuring It Out)

So, I started my career as a data analyst—because spreadsheets seemed like a good idea at the time. But then I realised I was better at making things look good than making them add up.

Fast forward, and here I am, a marketing and business guy, pursuing IB & E at Symbiosis, and while I don't have it all figured out, I'm definitely more fun to work with than a spreadsheet.

And yes, my grandma is still convinced I work in IT.

My Philosophy.

I believe that the best marketing comes from a mix of data, creativity, and a lot of “let's see what happens.” You can have all the data in the world, but without a little bit of magic, it's just numbers.

I'm passionate about helping brands grow, even if it means explaining it to my grandma with hand gestures and a flowchart.
